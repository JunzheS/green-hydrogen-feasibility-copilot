## National Policy Framework

- **SNH II (National Hydrogen Strategy)**: Targets 4.5 GW of electrolysis by 2030, updated 2024
- **France 2030**: EUR 7 billion allocated to hydrogen
- **IPCEI Hydrogen**: French projects including H2V Dunkerque, Normand'Hy, and Green Horizon
- **ZIBAC**: Low-carbon industrial zones in Dunkirk, Le Havre, Fos-sur-Mer
- **Hydrogen Valleys**: French government supports 12+ hydrogen valley projects
- **ADEME**: Supporting decarbonized hydrogen production through tender mechanisms

## Key Policy Advantages

| Advantage | Impact |
