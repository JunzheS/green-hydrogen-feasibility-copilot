# Cost Data Collection Strategy — Sprint 1

**Document:** Source Identification & Prioritization
**Date:** 2026-06-05
**Author:** Senior Cost Engineer & Hydrogen Project Economist
**Purpose:** Define source hierarchy for Cost Library Sprint 1 (30 records)

---

## 1. Source Hierarchy

| Level | Definition | % Target | Typical Use |
|-------|-----------|---------|-------------|
| **A** | Official developer/OEM disclosures, government filings | 20% | Electrolyzer stack cost, grid connection cost, total CAPEX from Gold Dataset |
| **B** | IEA, IRENA, Hydrogen Council, EU reports | 60% | Category-level benchmarks, cost breakdowns, learning rates |
| **C** | Industry media, trade journals, analyst reports | 20% | Supplementary confirmation, technology trends, cost driver analysis |
| **D** | EXCLUDED — per Source Governance Framework | 0% | — |
